Why Stories Matter

June 14, 2025

In a world flooded with information, noise, and endless scrolling, stories still cut through the clutter. They stick. They shape us. They stay with us when facts fade. At Bricks First Publishing, we believe stories are more than entertainment—they are a foundation. A brick. Something solid you can build a life on.

Stories Are How We’re Wired

Science and Scripture both agree: humans are storytelling creatures. Our brains aren’t built for bullet points—they’re built for narratives. When Jesus taught, He used parables. When a child asks for a bedtime story, they’re not just asking to pass the time—they're asking for meaning. Embedded in every story is a lesson, a warning, a hope, or a mirror.

Neuroscience confirms this. Stories activate more of the brain than mere data. They help us remember, empathize, and take action. In short, stories move us. Literally and figuratively.

Stories Shape Culture

Every civilization is built on stories. Some true. Some twisted. Some told so many times they become the lens through which entire generations see the world. Think about it: the stories we tell ourselves determine how we vote, how we lead, how we raise our kids, and how we view right and wrong.

That’s why at Bricks First, we take storytelling seriously. Because the right story at the right time can shift the direction of a person’s life—and even the culture at large.

Stories Help Us Heal

There’s a reason memoirs and testimonies feel sacred. When someone shares their journey—especially the messy parts—it gives others permission to be honest too. Stories remind us we’re not alone. They help us process trauma, frame redemption, and cast vision for a better future.

This is especially powerful in stories of recovery, faith, and personal transformation. When we see characters—or real people—face darkness and emerge stronger, it awakens courage in us. We begin to believe, maybe I can too.

Stories Build Legacy

Your great-grandfather may be gone, but if his story is told, he still speaks. Stories outlive us. They pass on wisdom, values, and identity. Long after we’re gone, our stories—written, spoken, or lived—can still lead others.

That’s why publishing matters. Writing matters. Reading matters. When we put good stories into the world, we build something that lasts.

Stories Are Bricks

At Bricks First, we’re not interested in fluff. We don’t publish clickbait or filler. We create and curate books that make a positive impact on people’s lives. Books that carry weight. Books that serve as bricks—foundations for better thinking, living, and leading.

Whether it’s a fantasy novel packed with purpose, a theological exploration that stirs your spirit, or a practical guide that moves you to action—our goal is the same: to tell stories that matter.

So why do stories matter?
Because stories build people. And people build the future.

If you're looking for stories that actually mean something—books that spark change, stir faith, and strengthen character—you’re in the right place.

Welcome to Bricks First Publishing. Let’s build something that lasts.
